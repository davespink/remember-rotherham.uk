Remember Rotherham

Column

Here we are in April 2025 and the world is burning, or so it seems.Trump has set the light with trade wars, and the stock makert is in free fall. The war lingers on in Ukraine, and Britain is in a state of anxiety, with deminished tfust in the Starmer government. Hot tops are the economy, immigration, lack of free speech, goverment invasion of privacy. Resentments that have lingered on for years are coming to a head. In particular the government's reluctance to open a new enquiry into what has been come to be known as "the Rotherham Grooming Gang scandal", or other similar words.

The purpose of this website is to act as a resource for links and material relevant to the scandal. It also will stand as a memorial, so that people never forget what happened in a northern steel town, after the reduction of the steel industry. It's called remember Rotherham because unhappily that wars one of the first cases to come to light. But is was afterwards found to be rampant and endemic to similar post industrial town in the north. There should be a Remember Telford, Remember Oxford, any number of towns up and down the city. I hope people will make their own sites and try to keep the memory alive. The victims have been treated scandalously buy their abusers, buy the police, by justice system, and by their own government. Stains on the national conscience.
